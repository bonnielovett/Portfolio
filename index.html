The story behind the work

Meaningful places, material experiments, and moments of joy.

Bonnie’s practice pays homage to the places and experiences that shape her world. From the quiet motion of water at her high school rowing club to shared conversations in gallery spaces, she layers memory, collaboration, and play into every piece.

Her process welcomes experimentation with materials, finding unexpected possibilities in how they behave. This approach creates work that feels both grounded and imaginative—a balance of heartfelt narrative and refined visual presence.

Anchor points

  • Studio research rooted in sculpture, fiber, and installation
  • Works that draw from outdoors, memory, and shared delight
  • Engagement with curation, gallery programming, and community collaboration
“I’m drawn to revisiting past moments and transforming them into works that honor memory while creating new experiences for others.”
